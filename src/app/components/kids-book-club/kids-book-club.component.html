This is a book club where we read kid's book and have a Very Serious Debate over them.

If you want to join, check the list below for the next Kid's Book Club Debate. Read the next book and attend the debate, or contact us if you want to work with a team captain to be more involved!

Everyone starts out the debate on either side of the issue, or neutral. All sides will have a captain who will make a presentation for their side. Anyone will be able to make arguments. During the debate everyone but the captain can switch sides. Whichever side has most people at the end wins!
